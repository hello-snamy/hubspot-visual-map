## The Eco-Friendly Manufacturing Reality

You’re not just selling a product. You’re selling:

* Sustainability credentials
* Certifications & compliance
* Long-term supply reliability
* Measurable environmental impact

So HubSpot must track **buyers, products, certifications, audits, and impact metrics** — all in one system.

---

## 1️⃣ Entry Points — How Sustainable Leads Come In

**Top-left of the map**

### Inbound

* Website inquiry / RFQ
* Sustainability landing pages
* ESG procurement portals
* Certifications & compliance listings
* Trade fairs / green expos

### Outbound

* Enterprise procurement outreach
* Brand partnerships
* Distributor onboarding
* Government / institutional bids

**Critical eco-data captured early**

* Industry (FMCG, retail, pharma, food)
* Sustainability requirement (plastic-free, compostable, recycled)
* Certification required (FSC, BRC, ISO, GOTS, etc.)
* Volume & MOQ
* Geography / export requirements

> Rule: If sustainability requirements aren’t captured, the deal will stall later.

## 2️⃣ Core CRM Objects — Sustainable System of Record

**Center-left**

### Contacts

* Procurement
* Sustainability / ESG officers
* Compliance managers

### Companies (Very Important)

The buying decision is **company-driven**, often by committees.

Company properties:

* Industry
* ESG maturity
* Certification requirements
* Preferred materials
* Audit frequency

---

### Deals (Commercial Agreements)

For eco-manufacturers:

* **Deals = supply contracts, purchase orders, or long-term agreements**

Examples:

* Pilot order
* Annual supply contract
* Private-label manufacturing

> Rule: One deal = one commercial commitment.

---

### Products (Critical)

Products must include:

* Material type
* Recycled content %
* Carbon footprint (if available)
* Certification mapping
* End-of-life method (recyclable / compostable)

---

### Custom Objects (Highly Recommended)

* **Certifications**
* **Audits**
* **Impact Metrics**
* **Shipments**

This is where eco-manufacturers differentiate.

### Certification / Audit Pipeline

### Post-Sale Sequences

* Impact reporting

## 5️⃣ Automation & Rules — Protecting Credibility

**Under everything**

### Lead Scoring (Eco-Focused)

Score higher for:

* Clear sustainability goals
* High-volume buyers
* Long-term contracts
* Repeat buyers

Lower score for:

* One-off low-volume requests
* Unclear compliance requirements

---

### Lifecycle Stages (Eco-Adjusted)

Subscriber
→ Inquiry
→ Qualified Buyer
→ Pilot Customer
→ **Active Sustainable Client**
→ Long-Term Partner
→ Advocate / Case Study

---

### Routing Rules

* By industry
* By certification complexity
* By region
* By export vs domestic

---

### Deal Automation

* Compliance review stage → notify QA team
* Deal won → create production + shipment workflows
* Delivery completed → trigger impact report

> Rule: Sustainability proof must be automatic and consistent.
